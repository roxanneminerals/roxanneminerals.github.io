About

I like learning, making stuff, and collaborating with others to create change.

I am a co-organizer for PyLadies Twin Cities, a local chapter of a national organization of women who love programming in the Python Language. I attend regular meetings of PyMNtos, the local Python user group. I am also a member of Open Twin Cities, a local group that collaboratively uses and creates technology for social good.

In my day job, I am a research analyst (aka "data storyteller") at the BlueGreen Alliance, a national coalition of labor unions and environmental groups. I work on using data to show our members and allies how we can reduce pollution and greenhouse gas emissions while building a clean and efficient economy. I also know quite a bit aboutfrastructure and supply chains. I am currently building my technical data analysis skills using Python.

I'm also crafty, I like to ride bikes, and I enjoy hunting for fossils and fungi.
